SpeedVolume Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 1, 2025

Introduction

SpeedVolume respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

Data We Collect

Location Data

  • GPS coordinates and speed information to adjust volume based on your activity
  • Continuous location tracking during app use and in background when enabled
  • Speed calculations derived from location data

Music Data

  • Currently playing track information (title, artist, album) from authorized music services
  • Playback state (playing, paused, stopped)
  • Basic playback controls when you use app features

App Usage Data

  • Settings preferences and app configuration
  • Volume adjustment curves and thresholds
  • Music service authorization status
We do NOT collect:
  • Personal identification information
  • Complete music library contents
  • Audio recordings of any kind
  • Contact information or communications
  • Payment or financial information

How We Use Your Data

Location Data Usage

Location data is essential for SpeedVolume's core functionality:

  • Real-time speed monitoring during sports and activities
  • Automatic volume adjustment based on configured speed thresholds
  • Background processing to maintain functionality when app is not active
  • Speed calculations and trend analysis for optimal volume control
Important: Your location data is processed locally on your device in real-time and is not transmitted to external servers or stored permanently.

Music Data Usage

Music service integration enables our volume control features:

  • Displaying current track information in the app interface
  • Controlling music playback (play, pause, skip) when requested
  • Adjusting system volume based on your activity
  • Providing a seamless music control experience

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage

  • All settings and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Location data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • Music track information is cached temporarily for display purposes only

Cloud Synchronization

Some app settings may synchronize across your devices if you have iCloud enabled in your device settings. You can disable this through your iOS Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud.

Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for stored data
  • Secure communication with music service APIs
  • No data transmission to external servers beyond authorized music services
  • Regular security updates and best practices implementation

Third-Party Service Integration

SpeedVolume integrates with the following music services with your explicit consent:

Apple Music

  • Official Apple MediaPlayer framework integration
  • Access to currently playing track information
  • Playback control capabilities

Spotify

  • Official Spotify iOS SDK integration
  • Remote control of Spotify playback
  • Current track and playback state information
Third-Party Data Sharing: We share playback control commands and receive track information only from music services you explicitly authorize. No other data sharing occurs with unauthorized third parties.

Data Retention

  • Settings and Preferences: Stored until you delete the app or reset settings
  • Location Data: Processed in real-time, not stored beyond current session
  • Music Data: Cached temporarily during app session only
  • App Usage Data: Stored locally until app deletion

Your Privacy Rights

Access and Control

  • View Data: Review all collected data through the app's Settings
  • Modify Settings: Change or delete your preferences at any time
  • Revoke Permissions: Disable location or music access through iOS Settings > Privacy
  • Complete Deletion: Remove all app data by deleting the application

Exercise Your Rights

  • Use the app's Settings menu to modify preferences
  • Access iOS Settings > Privacy to manage permissions
  • Contact us for assistance with privacy-related requests
  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored data

Children's Privacy

SpeedVolume is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

International Users

If you are located outside the United States, please note that your data is processed locally on your device. No data is transmitted internationally to our servers.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will notify users through the app or via email if contact information is available. Your continued use of SpeedVolume after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
